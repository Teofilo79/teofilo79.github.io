This simple page gives you short intro on the situation in any city of the world - what is the temperature, latest news, upcoming holidays. I think it will be useful for "small talks" - open this before you call to your colleague in any part of the world, and you may have some ideas on wht to talk before you go business. At least I see it so. So the tool can be called also "Small Talks ideas" :)
<hr>
I would say coding went much faster than expected. Looked the code - it is readible, maintainable, and quite simple. The thing that may take you a week - was done in few hours... Indeed, the thing is that now you need to concentrate on creating correct prompts rather than learning new code features. You need to force yourself not to think on "how" and rather concentrate on "what I want to see in the end".

I used Claude, but probably will concentrate on Codex in future.
